FRESHSPAWN NIGHT-OUT SUCCESS FORECAST


Whilst we can assert with a reasonable degree of confidence that:

Cav + Freshspawns + drinking = fun

Volatire tells us that absolute certainty is an absurd proposition. To mitigate the likelihood of an underwhelming gathering, the predictive powers of this logarithm will enable the user to forsee possible scenarios and eventualities prior to a night-out.

The premise is that the well established Freshspawns have embarked on a day trip to the Cavendish bar in West Street, Sheffield. After multiple jars, an opportunity to introduce a number of unknowns presents itself.
